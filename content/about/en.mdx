# About dailycryptic

`dailycryptic` is focused on one core experience: a fast **daily cryptic** and **minute cryptic** clue workflow with clear learning support.

## What We Publish

- Publish one **minute cryptic** clue every day as our **daily cryptic**
- Offer **progressive hints** for guided solving
- Provide **answer checking** and clear explanation for each clue
- Maintain a **date-based archive** so players can practice older clues

## How Hints Work

Each clue includes 4 hint levels, revealed one at a time:

1. **Level 1** - broad solving direction
2. **Level 2** - mechanism hint (for example anagram or container)
3. **Level 3** - structure guidance
4. **Level 4** - targeted help

## Editorial Standards

### Clue Quality

- Natural surface reading
- Valid cryptic construction
- Clear split between definition and wordplay

### Difficulty Balance

- Balanced difficulty across the week
- Short sessions suitable for daily practice
- Explanations designed for learning, not only checking

## Corrections Policy

If you find an issue in a clue or explanation, send us:

- Puzzle date
- The issue you found
- Your reasoning or suggested correction

We review correction reports and update content when needed.
