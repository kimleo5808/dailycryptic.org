# About StrandsHint

`StrandsHint` is an independent fan site dedicated to helping players solve the daily NYT Strands puzzle. We provide progressive hints, full answers, and a complete archive of every puzzle since the game launched.

## What StrandsHint does

We operate a daily hint and answer service for the NYT Strands word puzzle:

- Publish **progressive hints** for each day's puzzle — from vague clues to near-giveaways, so you choose how much help you want
- Maintain a **complete archive** of every Strands puzzle with full answers, theme words, and Spangrams
- Provide **strategy guides and tips** to help you improve your solving skills
- Automatically update puzzle data daily so you always have the latest information

## How our hints work

Each puzzle includes a Spangram and several theme words. We provide up to five levels of hints:

1. **Hint Level 1** — A vague thematic clue to point you in the right direction
2. **Hint Level 2** — A more specific hint that narrows down the theme
3. **Hint Level 3** — Word length information to help you identify words on the grid
4. **Hint Level 4** — Starting letters of each theme word are revealed
5. **Hint Level 5** — Full answers including the Spangram and all theme words

This progressive system lets you get just enough help to stay challenged without fully spoiling the puzzle.

## Our data sources

Puzzle data is collected automatically from publicly available community sources and cross-referenced for accuracy. Our crawler runs multiple times daily to ensure we have the latest puzzle data as soon as it becomes available.

## What makes StrandsHint different

- **No spoilers by default** — All answers are hidden behind reveal buttons so you never accidentally see the solution
- **Progressive hints** — Unlike sites that just show answers, we give you graduated clues to preserve the puzzle experience
- **Complete archive** — Every puzzle searchable and browsable by date
- **Clean design** — No pop-ups, no autoplay videos, no fake countdown timers. Just the hints and answers you need

## Content scope and limitations

This site provides hints, answers, and educational content related to the NYT Strands puzzle. We do not provide access to the actual game — you can play the official puzzle at [nytimes.com/games/strands](https://www.nytimes.com/games/strands).
