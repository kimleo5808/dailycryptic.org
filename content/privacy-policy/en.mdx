# Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how `dailycryptic.org` ("dailycryptic", "we", "us") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our site.

## 1. Who We Are

Controller: dailycryptic.org  
Contact: `hello@dailycryptic.org`

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Usage and Device Data

We may collect page views, browser type, device type, referral source, region-level location, and timestamps.

### 2.2 Technical and Security Logs

We may collect service logs needed for security monitoring, abuse prevention, and performance diagnostics.

### 2.3 Contact Information

If you email us, we collect the information you provide in your message.

We do not provide paid subscriptions and we do not collect payment card information.

## 3. Analytics and Advertising Services

In production, we may use:

- Vercel Analytics
- Google Analytics
- Plausible Analytics (if enabled)
- Google AdSense (if enabled)

These services may process data according to their own policies.

## 4. Cookies and Similar Technologies

We and our providers may use cookies or similar technologies for:

- Site operation and security
- Traffic and performance measurement
- Advertising delivery and measurement (if ads are enabled)

Where required by law, we request consent before non-essential cookies are used.

## 5. How We Use Information

We use information to:

- Operate and improve the website
- Protect the service from abuse
- Analyze usage trends
- Respond to support and correction requests
- Comply with legal obligations

We do not sell personal data for money.

## 6. Sharing of Information

We may share data with hosting, analytics, advertising, and security providers that support site operations.  
We may also share data when required by law or to protect legal rights.

## 7. Data Retention

- Security and technical logs: generally up to 90 days unless longer retention is required
- Support communications: as needed to resolve requests and keep records
- Aggregated analytics: based on provider settings and operational need

## 8. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or request portability of your personal data, and to object to certain processing.

To submit a request, contact `hello@dailycryptic.org`.

## 9. Children's Privacy

`dailycryptic.org` is a general-audience site and is not directed to children under 13.  
We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## 10. Third-Party Services and Links

Our site may include links to third-party services and websites.  
Their privacy practices are governed by their own policies.

## 11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.  
The "Updated on" date at the top reflects the latest revision.
